Planet Details

Mercury

Mercury is the smallest and closest planet to the Sun in the Solar System. It has no satellites and no significant atmosphere.

  • Distance from Sun: 0.39 AU
  • Orbital Period: 88 days
  • Diameter: 4,879 km
  • Number of Satellites: 0

Venus

Venus is the second planet in the Solar System and the closest planetary neighbor to Earth.

  • Distance from Sun: 0.72 AU
  • Orbital Period: 225 days

Earth

Earth is the third planet in the Solar System and currently the only known celestial body that supports life.

  • Distance from Sun: 1.00 AU
  • Orbital Period: 365.25 days
