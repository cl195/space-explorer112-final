The Sun

The Sun is the star at the center of our Solar System. It is a nearly perfect sphere of hot plasma, with internal convective motion that generates a magnetic field.

Composition

The Sun is primarily composed of hydrogen (about 74%) and helium (about 24%).

Hydrogen 74%
Helium 24%

Temperature

The Sun's surface temperature is about 5,500°C, while its core reaches 15 million°C.
